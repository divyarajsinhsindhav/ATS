About the System

An Application Tracking System aimed at ensuring efficient governance within Rajkot Gram and Jilla Panchayat. This system intends to streamline the process of handling applications, requests, and services offered by the local government, providing transparency and accountability.

Our Services

Our system offers the following services:

  • Application submission and tracking
  • Transparent approval process
  • Efficient service delivery
  • Feedback and grievance redressal
